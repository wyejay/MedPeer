Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 30, 2025

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Registration Information: Name, email, username, password, professional role, institution, specialty
  • Profile Information: Bio, profile picture, location, website links
  • Content: Posts, comments, messages, uploaded files
  • Communications: Messages sent through our contact forms or support channels

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, time spent, clicks, search queries
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Log Data: Server logs, error reports, performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Authenticate users and prevent fraud
  • Facilitate communication between users
  • Send important notifications and updates
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Enforce our terms of service

3. Information Sharing

3.1 Public Information

Information you choose to make public (based on your privacy settings) may be visible to other users:

  • Public profile information
  • Posts and comments (based on privacy settings)
  • Professional credentials and affiliations

3.2 Third Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Email delivery, file storage, analytics (under strict confidentiality)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect safety
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition (with notice)

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure file storage and handling
  • Regular backup and recovery procedures

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Account Controls

  • Profile Visibility: Control who can see your profile (Public, Followers, Private)
  • Content Sharing: Choose privacy levels for posts and content
  • Communication: Control who can message you
  • Notifications: Manage email and in-app notifications

5.2 Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data

6. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • Active Accounts: As long as your account remains active
  • Deleted Accounts: Up to 90 days for recovery, then permanently deleted
  • Legal Requirements: As required by applicable laws
  • Aggregated Data: May be retained indefinitely in anonymized form

7. Medical Information and HIPAA

Important Medical Privacy Notice
  • Never share patient-identifying information on MedPeer
  • MedPeer is not a HIPAA-covered entity
  • Do not use the platform for patient care decisions
  • Discuss cases only in de-identified, educational contexts
  • Consult your institution's policies on social media use

8. International Users

MedPeer may transfer and store data in countries different from your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

9. Children's Privacy

MedPeer is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email or platform notification at least 30 days in advance.

11. Contact Us

  • Mailing Address: [Company Address]

12. Your Consent

By using MedPeer, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use our services.
